# Limitations

This project is a controlled research prototype.

It does not claim to solve the general version of the research problem.  
The purpose is to provide a small, inspectable, reproducible experimental environment where the mechanism can be tested quantitatively.

## Main limitations

- The environments are synthetic.
- The true causal structure is known by construction.
- The models are intentionally lightweight.
- The results should be interpreted as evidence of a mechanism, not as a general benchmark result.
- Scaling to real data would require stronger model classes, stronger baselines, and more rigorous experimental design.

## Why this is still useful

A controlled setting allows:

- known ground truth,
- reproducible metrics,
- clear failure analysis,
- focused investigation of the mechanism.
